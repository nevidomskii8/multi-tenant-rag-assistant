# Account and Login

Each Cloudberry user signs in with an email address and password, or through a
linked Google or Microsoft account. If you registered with an email and password
and later linked a social account, either method will work for the same profile.

To reset a forgotten password, click "Forgot password" on the sign-in screen and
enter your account email. A reset link is sent that stays valid for one hour; if
it expires, simply request a new one. For security, resetting the password signs
out every active session on all devices, so you will need to sign in again
everywhere.

Two-factor authentication (2FA) is optional but recommended. Once enabled under
Settings → Security, each sign-in requires a six-digit code from an authenticator
app. If you lose access to your authenticator, the one-time recovery codes shown
when you enabled 2FA let you sign in and disable it. Without a recovery code, a
workspace administrator must reset your 2FA from the member management page.
