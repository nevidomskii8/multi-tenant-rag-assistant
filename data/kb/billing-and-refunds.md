# Billing and Refunds

Cloudberry bills every workspace on a monthly cycle. The billing date is the day
you first upgraded to a paid plan, and each invoice is charged automatically to
the card on file. You can view past invoices under Settings → Billing → History,
where every charge is listed with its date, plan tier, and applied taxes.

Refunds are available within 14 days of a charge for annual plans and within 7
days for monthly plans. To request a refund, open a ticket from the Billing page
and include the invoice number. Approved refunds are returned to the original
payment method and usually appear within five business days, depending on your
bank. Partial refunds are issued when you downgrade mid-cycle: the unused portion
of the higher tier is prorated and credited to your next invoice.

If a payment fails, Cloudberry retries the charge three times over six days
before suspending the workspace. During suspension the data is retained but the
workspace becomes read-only. Updating the card on file and clearing the balance
restores full access immediately, with no loss of data.
